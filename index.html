NASA SPACE APPS

NATIONAL WINNERS

+
+

In 2025, our team at Fixare Studio achieved a remarkable milestone by winning the NASA Space Apps Challenge at the national level, showcasing our expertise in space technology and innovative problem-solving.

Our winning project, "Voro Nova", was developed during a 48-hour hackathon where we tackled one of NASA's most challenging problem statements. The solution leveraged cutting-edge AI and data visualization to help scientists better understand cosmic phenomena.

As the National Winners, we demonstrated exceptional technical prowess in space data analysis, user experience design, and cross-platform development. Our solution stood out among hundreds of entries for its practical application of machine learning algorithms to process vast amounts of space data in real-time.

This achievement not only highlights our technical capabilities but also our commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in space technology and data science. The recognition from NASA has opened new opportunities for our team to collaborate with space agencies and research institutions worldwide.
